OB52 Highlights

Jujutsu Kaisen Collaboration

Collect Cursed Energy in BR to unlock iconic techniques and tools, plus themed areas and special interactions across modes.

Includes unlockable Jujutsu like Divergent Fist, Hollow Purple, Domain Expansions, and more (event rules apply).

New BR Location: Neon City

Enter via Rift Portals, explore a nighttime metropolis, then fast travel back out to different areas of the map.

New Character: Morse

Stealth Bytes enables stealth flanks and safer utility actions (revive, vending, vehicle moves) without being easily spotted.

BR: Breakable Arsenal + Ultra Airdrop

Smash into arsenals for high-value loot (but it triggers a reveal), and fight for a capture-based Ultra Airdrop in late game.

CS: Cyber Airdrop Optimization

Cyber Airdrops now reward contested captures with staged progress and a damage reduction buff to improve counterplay.

System: Camera + Highlight Photos + FPS

New camera tools, auto-captured highlight moments, and experimental frame rate boosts for smoother play.

Jujutsu Awakening (Event Summary)

Battle Royale

  • Cursed Energy piles across maps; collect by proximity, eliminations, or vending.
  • Spend energy to unlock Jujutsu and a Cursed Tool via a dedicated panel.
  • Key unlocks include Divergent Fist, Hollow Purple, Ember Insects, and Domain Expansions like Unlimited Void / Malevolent Shrine.
  • Revival Points are imbued with Cursed Energy for faster return-to-fight pace.

Treat this as a “hot drop” meta patch: expect volatile late-game swings when Jujutsu unlocks come online.

Official collaboration art.

Clash Squad

  • Jujutsu techniques (incl. Hollow Purple, Divergent Fist, Ember Insects, and Domain Expansions) appear in CS Store and Cyber Airdrops.
  • Jujutsu-themed Cyber Airdrops + spawn points; themed Peak area also appears in CS.
  • Training Grounds allows trying Jujutsu (except Unlimited Void) and Cursed Tools.

Bermuda High School (Peak)

Bermuda’s Peak is renovated into a themed “High School” area for the event — expect rerouted fights and new cover lines.

Good for content: wide angles + recognizable POI shots.

Characters

Morse — New Character

Stealth Bytes (Active) puts you into Stealth Mode for smart flanks and safer utility actions.

  • Hard to see beyond 16m, cannot be detected/scanned, and gains 20% movement speed.
  • Aim assist triggers if enemies are within 4m.
  • Duration up to 15s; cannot fire while stealthed; 1s exit delay.
  • Cooldown: 45s.

Caster angle: “Morse enables stealth timings—watch for late-round backstabs and stealth resets into revive/vending plays.”

Skill Balance (Quick Notes)

  • Tatsuya: cooldown reduced; reset now only if knockdown happens within 10s after activation.
  • Wukong: similar reset rule adjustment + cooldown reduction.
  • Xayne: stronger shields + longer duration for aggressive pushes.
  • Nero: “better control” (casting/handling optimization noted in official summary art).

Official character update art.

Weapons & Balancing

Core Changes

  • Weapon rarity colors added for quick tier readability.
  • Explosive weapons capacity reduced (FGL-24 / RGS-50: 3 → 2).
  • Upgradeable weapons: Kar98k and FAMAS upgrade mechanism removed (now match their “III” attributes in practice).

Airdrop Buffs

  • Groza: damage +3%, armor pen +10%, range +8%.
  • SVD: accuracy +20%.
  • M249: rate of fire +5%, armor pen +5%.
  • Thompson: rate of fire +5%.

Other Notables

  • MAC10 family: damage +8%, accuracy +15%.
  • VSS: armor pen +10%, accuracy +5%.
  • Heal Pistol: auto-target teammates; ROF +30% with adjusted heal economy (incl. Heal Pistol-Y hitting 2 allies).

Meta read: expect more airdrop priority again, plus stronger “support heal” consistency with auto-targeting.

Official weapon update art.

Clash Squad

Cyber Airdrop Optimization

  • Two-stage Cyber Airdrop with shared capture progress.
  • At first-stage completion, players within the capture area receive rewards.
  • Damage reduction buff inside the zone; second-stage unlocker keeps the buff for the rest of the round.

Discounted Weapons

  • CS store can randomly offer weapons at a discounted price.

Other Adjustments

  • Special revive HP-bar effects added (items/skills).
  • Dropping USP at round start stops auto-issue in later rounds.
  • Smoke Vortex duration (Tactical Market loadout) reduced to 40s.

Official OB52 “new mechanism” art (captures the patch’s pacing focus).

Battle Royale

Breakable Arsenal

  • Arsenal glass can be cracked/shattered to force entry.
  • Breaking in triggers an Arsenal UAV that reveals your position.
  • Updated arsenal weapon sets + added Lv.3 backpacks; reduced upgrade/unlock cost (1500 → 1200 FF coins).

Ultra Airdrop

  • Late-game capture-based device.
  • Rewards high-end buffs for teams willing to contest.

Revival + Rating

  • New/updated revival mechanism (mode-dependent) and revive quick messages.
  • Rating medals during BR; strong team actions raise rating and grant bonus rank points.

Loot / QoL

  • Start with Lv.2 backpack.
  • Request-to-be-jumpmaster + improved drop prompts.
  • High-value item markers color-coded by tier.
  • Weapon rack pool updated; vest distribution adjusted (more Lv.2, less Lv.1, added Lv.3 helms/vests).

Vehicles

  • Brief speed boost feature.
  • Explosion behavior + wreck cover optimizations.

Official BR battlefield update art.

Map

Neon City

  • Enter via any of 4 Rift Portals on the map.
  • Inside the city, use portals to fast travel to other areas of the map.
  • No fighting inside Neon City; max stay 30s before returning.

Other Map Adjustments

  • Kalahari windows smoother vaulting; damage registration bug fixed in some spots.
  • Kalahari CS balance tweaks in multiple areas.
  • Bermuda: added themed hair salon.
  • Solara: improved slide interactions and speed.

Official Neon City art.

System & Social

Camera System (Big Update)

  • Character Lock + Camera Lock for flexible angles.
  • New filters, multi-camera templates, and improved interaction templates.
  • Option to display enemies in Camera System (Casual Custom Room).

Highlight Photos

  • Auto screenshots for key moments (ACE, streaks, headshots).
  • View on results page + Profile Album tab (storage limited).

Frame Rate Enhancement

  • Experimental frame rate boost option under High FPS Mode.

Broadcast tip: highlight photos are easy “instant replay” references for social posts.

Official system update art.

OB51 Highlights

New Character: Nero

Nero brings a tactical buff that helps squads push or hold by converting Gloo usage into temporary combat advantages. Great for coordinated rushes and late-circle skirmishes.

New Guns: Winchester & Thompson-X

Classic Winchester returns reworked for mid-range precision. Thompson-X is a controllable SMG built for aggressive entry fragging.

Loadout Rework

Team utilities like Team Booster, Enhance Hammer, Tactical Market, and Super Leg Pockets streamline BR setup with clearer choices and stronger identities.

BR Quality-of-Life

Waypoint pings, improved Weapon Rack info, and new utility items such as Gloo Mushroom and Gloo UAV-Lite make rotations and scouting more readable.

Clash Squad Tuning

Purchase flow refinements, Gloo and grenade adjustments, and pacing tweaks to reduce early snowball and encourage counterplay.

Solara Map Balance

Cover, loot, and landing adjustments across key POIs to increase fair fights and diversify rotations.

OB51 Characters

Nero — Featured Character

Nero’s skill rewards smart utility usage and team play. It triggers a short-window buff around Gloo interactions so your squad can breach, trade, and reset more safely. Best paired with entry SMGs/shotguns and a support running extra Gloo.

  • Pairs well with aggressive brawlers and revive-centric comps.
  • Synergizes with utility-heavy rotations and late-circle holds.
